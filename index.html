About Our Project

We believe that all teens should be able to learn how to finance, save, spend, and budget easily. Through interactive tools, clear explanations, and a friendly AI chatbot, we make financial literacy simple and engaging. Whether you're earning your first paycheck or saving up for a goal, we're here to guide you every step of the way.

Accessible

We make this site accessible to all, allowing for teens being able to learn, log, and improve on their financial skills and abilities.

Teaching

We teach teens what financing, budgeting, saving, and investing is. We provide quizzes to test and improve their abilities. We also offer outside resources and oppertunities to grow such as how to start investing.

Enable

We enable teens to be able to make their own decisions. We provide them with resources to help them be able to learn, try out, and finally enable them to do things by themselves.
